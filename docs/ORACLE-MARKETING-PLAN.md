## Part 3: Psychological Marketing Tricks for the Blog

### Cialdini’s Principles (You’re Already Using Some)

| Principle | How to Apply to Oracle Blog |
|-----------|----------------------------|
| **Reciprocity** | Lead with value: full prompts, rituals, frameworks — then mention app as tool, not destination |
| **Authority** | Jung citations, neuroscience links, "lab-backed" framing |
| **Social Proof** | "People who journal know…" / testimonials / "X readers started shadow work this week" |
| **Liking** | Confessional tone, founder voice, "I built this because I couldn't find…" |
| **Commitment** | "If you've read this far…" — micro-commitments; quiz before download |
| **Scarcity** | "Limited spots" for beta features, waitlist for new prompt packs |
| **Unity** | "Those of us who do inner work…" — in-group language |

### Additional Psychological Tactics

| Tactic | Mechanism | Implementation |
|--------|-----------|----------------|
| **Curiosity Gap** | Headlines that promise a revelation without giving it away | "The One Question My AI Journal Asked That Changed Everything" |
| **Open Loops** | End sections with unanswered questions; reader returns for resolution | "Next week: what your dream symbols really mean" |
| **Identity-Based Copy** | "You're the kind of person who…" | "You're the kind of person who needs a space for your messiest thoughts" |
| **Loss Aversion** | Frame in terms of what they're losing by not journaling | "What Your Unprocessed Emotions Are Costing You" |
| **Pattern Interrupt** | Unexpected format (lists, quizzes, "confessions") | "3 Things I Wish I'd Known Before Starting Shadow Work" |
| **Nostalgia** | "Remember when journaling was just for you?" | Position Oracle as "journaling before the algorithm" |
| **Variable Reward** | Keep them coming back | "New prompt every Monday" — creates anticipation |
| **Foot-in-the-Door** | Small ask before big ask | "Try one prompt" → "Start your 7-day shadow work series" |
| **Endowment Effect** | Give something free first | Free PDF "30 Shadow Work Prompts" → they feel they "own" the relationship |
| **Anchoring** | Show "before" state to make "after" feel dramatic | "Before I had a place for my shadow work, I…" |

### Headline Formulas That Convert

- *"Why [Common Belief] Is Keeping You Stuck"*
- *"The [Number] [Noun] That [Outcome]"* — e.g. "The One Prompt That Unlocked My Anxiety"
- *"I Tried [Thing] for [Time] — Here's What Happened"*
- *"[Surprising Fact] About [Topic] (And What to Do)"*
- *"Stop [Bad Habit]. Start [Simple Alternative]."*

- **Palette**: Warm browns, soft golds, muted greens — "Soft Life" coded

2. For each new article: one hero image + one Pinterest card (quote graphic).

4. Apply a consistent filter/overlay (warm, slightly desaturated) across all.
5. Store a copy in the repo (e.g. `assets/blog/` or `content/posts/images/`) so the build can reference them.

## Part 6: TikTok & Reels Strategy — The Full Plan

### The Core Philosophy: Mood Board, Not Marketing

The account should feel like a **personal digital diary** or a **curated mood board** — not a brand account. Gen Z scrolls past anything that smells like advertising. The goal is to make people *save* the post, not just like it.

**Golden rule**: If it looks like an ad, they won't watch. If it looks like a vibe, they'll share it.

### 2026 Aesthetics to Lead With

| Aesthetic | What It Is | How Oracle Uses It |
|-----------|------------|-------------------|
| **Frutiger Aero** | Tech-meets-nature; glossy UI over organic textures | App screenshots overlaid on nature shots, soft gradients |
| **Soft Productivity** | Anti-hustle; gentle routines, cozy rituals | "Slow morning" posts, candle-lit desk setups |
| **Dreamcore** | Liminal spaces, ethereal imagery, surreal calm | Cosmic/astrology posts, Oracle's existing UI vibe |
| **Dark Academia Lite** | Books, warm lighting, handwriting, introspection | Shadow work posts, Jung quotes, old book imagery |

### Content Format: Aesthetic Photo + Text-on-Photo (Carousel or Static)

Every post = **one photo + one line of text on screen**. No voiceover required. No face required (at first). Think: Pinterest meets TikTok.

#### Priority 2: Technical Performance

These are quick wins that improve rankings and user experience.

| Fix | Impact | Effort | Details |
|-----|--------|--------|---------|
| Add `defer` to script.js | Core Web Vitals (FCP) | 5 min | `<script src="/script.js" defer>` |

| Convert images to WebP | LCP, page weight | 1 hr | Use `sharp` or `cwebp`; keep PNG fallback |

| Tool | Privacy Level | What You Get |
|------|-------------|--------------|
